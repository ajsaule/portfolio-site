About me

I have a diverse background, from my humble beginnings working for smaller retail companies, to working in operations at a high volume money exchange alongside several other roles in web development, developing web and mobile applications for clients. Throughout this time I grew an understanding of the unique challenges of both small business and larger scale companies.

Since the COVID pandemic hit it has been extremely important for businesses to have a presence online, so I started helping some of my local businesses that didn't have one by creating a custom built webpage for them and optmising their SEO.

I was able to successfully develop digital presence for two local businesses, one being one of Melbournes oldest family medical clinics which I successfully grew web traffic 140x times from when we first started working together, I was also able to promote their website organically through some SEO techniques and persistent optimisation of their webpage to get them ranking #5 on Google organic search results over the time we have been working together. This lead to an increase in walk in patients at the clinic and the client was very happy with the result.

Some of the services I offer:

  • Building webpages from scratch with custom design and features based on your needs
  • Implementing a customised backend administration application to fit your requirements
  • SEO optimisation to boost organic traffic to your webpage
  • Ongoing monthly maintenance or ad hoc feature additions to your site
  • Professional photos of your business to use as web/ business assets

I also offer bespoke service packages based on your needs so feel free to ask!
