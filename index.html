About me

Since childhood I've been an avid fan of technology, constantly tinkering, upgrading, and seeking new ways to modify my devices whether it be remote control cars or desktop computers. I have always wanted to translate these interests into something more impactful.

This is one of the reasons why I took the leap into a career of software engineering, it felt very natural to me given my interests and previous experiences working in agile tech companies, alongside software development teams. Though as natural as it may have felt there were many challenges I had to overcome on this journey, including the fact that we were in a global pandemic.

I found the process of building things from the ground up and contributing to existing repositories on GitHub a very rewarding and energising experience. The fact that you can openly contribute to someone's project on the other side of the globe from the comfort of your own couch is so awesome.

As an individual helping people solve problems brings me joy, this is something I have developed a knack for throughout my career. I am also quite analytical in my work and am able to utilise my resourcefulness to tackle the more difficult tasks.
