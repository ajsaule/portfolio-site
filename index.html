About me

Since childhood I've been an avid fan of technology, always tinkering, upgrading and seeking new ways to modify my devices - whether it be remote control cars or desktop computers.

This recent leap into a career of software engineering has felt very natural to me given my interests and previous experiences working in tech companies alongside software development teams in an agile environment. Among the many challenges I faced on this coding journey, I found the process of building things from the ground up and contributing to existing repositories on GitHub a very rewarding and energising experience.

The 2020 COVID crisis has presented a very challenging period for humanity globally, personally during this time I have focused on up-skilling and embarking on this career change to give myself new opportunities in a space where I feel my existing experience and newly developed skills will fit well.
