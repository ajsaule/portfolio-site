About me

I have a diverse background, from my humble beginnings working from working for smaller retail companies, working in operations at a high demand money exchange and having several roles within web development and developing higher level web and mobile application for clients. Throughout this time I grew an understanding of the unique challenges of both small business and larger scale companies.

Recently since the COVID pandemic hit, I started helping some of my local businesses to boost their traffic and online presence, we all know how valuable it is to have a digital footprint in the current economic climate.

I was able to successfully digital presence for two local business, one being one of Melbournes oldest family medical clinics which I successfully grew web traffic 42 times from when we first started working together, this business did have established social media presence but I was able to promote their website organically through some SEO techniques and persistent optimisation of their webpage over the time we have been working together.

Some of the services I offer:

  • Building webpages from scratch with custom design and features based on your needs
  • Implementing a customised backend administration application to fit your requirements
  • SEO optimisation to boost organic traffic to your webpage
  • Ongoing monthly maintenence or ad hoc feature additions to your site
  • Professional photos of your business to use as web/ business assets

I also offer bespoke service packages based on your needs so feel free to ask!
